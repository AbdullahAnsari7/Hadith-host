🤝 Hadith of the Day

📖 Hadith (Arabic)

مَا زَالَ جِبْرِيلُ يُوصِينِي بِالْجَارِ حَتَّى ظَنَنْتُ أَنَّهُ سَيُوَرِّثُهُ

📘 English Translation

The Prophet ﷺ said: “Jibreel (Gabriel) kept advising me concerning the neighbor to the extent that I thought he would make him an heir.”

📚 Reference

Sahih al-Bukhari (6014) & Sahih Muslim (2625)

🌱 Moral of the Hadith

Islam emphasizes kindness and care towards neighbors. Being good to our neighbors strengthens community bonds and is a sign of true faith.
