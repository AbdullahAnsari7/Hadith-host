🌙 Hadith of the Day

📖 Hadith

لَا يُؤْمِنُ أَحَدُكُمْ حَتَّى يُحِبَّ لِأَخِيهِ مَا يُحِبُّ لِنَفْسِهِ

📘 English Translation

"None of you truly believes until he loves for his brother what he loves for himself."

📚 Reference

Sahih al-Bukhari, Hadith 13; Sahih Muslim, Hadith 45

🌱 Moral of the Hadith

This Hadith highlights that true Iman (faith) is not complete until a believer sincerely wishes for others the same goodness he desires for himself — in faith, character, and worldly matters.
