🌙 Hadith of the Day

📖 Hadith

بَلِّغُوا عَنِّي وَلَوْ آيَةً

📘 English Translation

"Convey from me, even if it is one verse."

📚 Reference

Sahih al-Bukhari, Hadith 3461

🌱 Moral of the Hadith

This Hadith reminds every Muslim to share the message of Islam — even a small piece of knowledge — with sincerity and wisdom. Each act of Da’wah can guide others and earn immense reward.
