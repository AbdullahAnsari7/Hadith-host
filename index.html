😊 Hadith of the Day

📖 Hadith (Arabic)

تَبَسُّمُكَ فِي وَجْهِ أَخِيكَ لَكَ صَدَقَةٌ

📘 English Translation

The Prophet ﷺ said: “Your smile for your brother is a charity (Sadaqah).”

📚 Reference

Jami‘ at-Tirmidhi — Hadith 1956

🌱 Moral of the Hadith

Islam teaches that even the smallest acts of kindness count as charity. A simple smile can spread joy, strengthen brotherhood, and earn immense reward from Allah ﷻ.
