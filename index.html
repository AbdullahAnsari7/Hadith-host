🌙 Hadith of the Day

📖 Hadith

تُعْرَضُ الأَعْمَالُ يَوْمَ الاِثْنَيْنِ وَالْخَمِيسِ، فَأُحِبُّ أَنْ يُعْرَضَ عَمَلِي وَأَنَا صَائِمٌ

📘 English Translation

"Deeds are presented (to Allah) on Mondays and Thursdays, and I love that my deeds be presented while I am fasting."

📚 Reference

Sunan al-Tirmidhi, Hadith 747

🌱 Moral of the Hadith

This Hadith encourages fasting on Mondays and Thursdays, as these are special days when deeds are presented before Allah ﷻ. Fasting on these days is a Sunnah that brings spiritual purification and closeness to Allah.
