🌙 Hadith of the Day

📖 Hadith

أَضَلَّ اللَّهُ عَنِ الْجُمُعَةِ مَن كَانَ قَبْلَنَا، فَكَانَ لِلْيَهُودِ يَوْمُ السَّبْتِ، وَكَانَ لِلنَّصَارَى يَوْمُ الْأَحَدِ، فَجَاءَ اللَّهُ بِنَا فَهَدَانَا لِيَوْمِ الْجُمُعَةِ.

📘 English Translation

"Allah led those before us astray from Friday. For the Jews, their day (of worship) was Saturday, and for the Christians, it was Sunday. Then Allah brought us and guided us to Friday."

📚 Reference

Sahih Muslim, Book 7, Hadith 31

🌱 Moral of the Hadith

This Hadith reminds us that Allah chose Friday as a special day of worship and gathering for the Muslim Ummah — a day of blessing, prayer, and unity, after previous nations were given Saturday and Sunday.
