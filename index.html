📜 Hadith of the Day

📖 Hadith

خَيْرُ يَوْمٍ طَلَعَتْ عَلَيْهِ الشَّمْسُ يَوْمُ الْجُمُعَةِ، فِيهِ خُلِقَ آدَمُ، وَفِيهِ أُدْخِلَ الْجَنَّةَ، وَفِيهِ أُخْرِجَ مِنْهَا

📘 English Translation

"The best day on which the sun has risen is Friday; on it Adam was created, on it he was admitted to Paradise, and on it he was expelled from it."

📚 Reference

Sahih Muslim, Book 7, Hadith 854

🌱 Moral of the Hadith

This Hadith highlights the virtue of Jumu‘ah, reminding us to honor this blessed day with extra worship, dhikr, and attending the Friday prayer.
