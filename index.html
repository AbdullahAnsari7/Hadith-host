📜 Hadith of the Day

📖 Hadith

إِنَّمَا الأَعْمَالُ بِالنِّيَّاتِ، وَإِنَّمَا لِكُلِّ امْرِئٍ مَا نَوَى

📘 English Translation

"Actions are judged by intentions, and every person will get the reward according to what they intended."

📚 Reference

Sahih al-Bukhari, Book 1, Hadith 1

🌱 Moral of the Hadith

This Hadith teaches that our sincerity and intentions are the foundation of all deeds. Allah looks at our hearts before our actions.
