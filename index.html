📜 Hadith of the Day

📖 Hadith

مَنْ عَادَ مَرِيضًا، أَوْ زَارَ أَخًا لَهُ فِي اللَّهِ، نَادَاهُ مُنَادٍ أَنْ طِبْتَ وَطَابَ مَمْشَاكَ، وَتَبَوَّأْتَ مِنَ الْجَنَّةِ مَنْزِلًا

📘 English Translation

"Whoever visits a sick person or visits his brother for the sake of Allah, a caller calls out: ‘May you be good and your walking be blessed, and may you take an abode in Paradise.’"

📚 Reference

Sunan al-Tirmidhi, Hadith 2008

🌱 Moral of the Hadith

This Hadith reminds us that visiting the sick is an act of mercy and love that brings immense reward — it strengthens bonds and earns the pleasure of Allah and a place in Jannah.
